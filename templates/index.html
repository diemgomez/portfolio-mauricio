My Journey

Hello! I'm Mauricio Gomez, an IT Support Specialist who is passionate about solving problems and helping people.

My journey into tech was sparked by a challenge at PriceSmart. Our team was stuck with slow, manual Excel reports, so I took the initiative to teach myself DOMO, an unused tool we had. I ended up automating our processes, turning hours of work into minutes, and discovering my passion for using technology to help a team work better.

To build on this foundation, I've since earned several professional certifications in IT Support and Cybersecurity.

I combine these technical skills with a strong background in customer service. As a fluent speaker of English, French, and Spanish, I am driven to provide clear, patient, and empathetic support to diverse teams. I'm currently seeking a challenging IT Support role where I can put my skills to work.
